How does Findit AI work?

Administrators at your organization take photos of found items and post them to the Findit AI app. Students, visitors, or staff can then browse the listings, claim their lost item, and arrange a pickup — all from their phone.

Who can use Findit AI?

Findit AI is built for schools, colleges, universities, transit authorities, offices, and any public space that handles lost items. If your organization runs a lost and found, Findit AI can make it better.

How do I get started?

Reach out through the form on this page or send us an email. We'll walk you through a quick onboarding process, set up your organization's account, and have you live on the app in no time.

What is the pricing like?

We offer flexible plans tailored to the size of your organization — from a single school campus to a large multi-site operation. Get in touch and we'll put together a plan that works for you.
