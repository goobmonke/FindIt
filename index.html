Traditional lost-and-found systems are slow, disorganized, and inefficient. They rely on physical logs, random lists, or manual searches that waste time and frustrate both staff and users. Lost items often go unclaimed due to inefficient or outdated systems, leading to frustration, wasted time, and unnecessary waste.

FindIt AI uses image recognition and natural language processing to match found item photos with user-submitted descriptions. Our solution transforms this process, making item recovery faster, easier, and more successful for schools, offices, gyms, and communities everywhere.
